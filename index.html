ABOUT ME


Hello! I'm Joseph Cook ("Joey" casually and "LummieThief" online), I use he/him pronouns, and I'm an indie game developer from Richland, WA. I'm currently studying Computer Science at the University of Washington in Seattle and am looking to turn game development into a career.

I love both making and playing games, and my favorite games tend to be team focused multiplayer games, brutally difficult games, and exploration games. That being said, it might not surprise you to hear some of my favorites are Outer Wilds, Spelunky 2, Deep Rock Galactic, Risk of Rain 2, League of Legends, and Minecraft.

I'm also a big fan of board games and have quite a large collection, with my favorites being Galaxy Trucker, Secret Hitler, Cockroach Poker, and The Search For Planet X.

In my free time I like to play games with my friends, work on personal projects, go running, listen to music, or solve puzzles. Puzzle solving is probably my favorite activity, and I think that's part of what drew me to game development, because as Mark Brown from Game Maker's Toolkit puts it: "Programming is basically the best puzzle game in the world."
